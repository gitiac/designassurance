About

21 years of delivery discipline across the UK, US, EU and APAC — now available as independent assurance

The problem we solve

Most infrastructure programmes don't fail in delivery — they fail in design. Flawed architectures, unvalidated low-level designs and unchallenged vendor proposals commit organisations to expensive, difficult-to-reverse problems before a single device is configured.

Design Assurance provides the independent technical scrutiny that large programmes rarely have. We review, validate and sign off designs across network, security and cloud environments — ensuring what gets built is what was agreed, and that it will actually work in production.

Where we add exceptional value is programme recovery. When a transformation has stalled — whether through poor architecture, vendor failure or delivery breakdown — we come in, diagnose quickly, and deliver.

Who we are

Design Assurance was founded by a CCDE and CCIE certified consultant with 21 years of hands-on experience across data centre, WAN and cloud transformation programmes. Our background spans highly regulated, high-security environments including financial services, pharmaceutical, healthcare, media, automotive and government sectors — with programme delivery across the UK, US, EU, APAC and Middle East.

We are contracted via leading global system integrators and specialist technology partners — trusted to deploy into their most complex and sensitive client engagements across the UK, US, EU, APAC and Middle East.

We are vendor-neutral. We have no allegiances to any technology provider and no vested interest in any particular outcome. Our only interest is in whether your design will work.

Common Questions
Do you work on-site or remotely?
Both. Design reviews are conducted remotely — accessible to clients across the UK, US, EU, APAC and Middle East. Programme recovery and ongoing assurance engagements may involve on-site presence depending on the programme's needs and location.
How quickly can you start?
For design reviews, typically within one to two weeks of engagement. For programme recovery, sooner where possible. Get in touch and we will discuss availability.
Do you sign NDAs?
Yes. All engagements are conducted under appropriate confidentiality agreements. We understand that programme information is commercially sensitive.
Can you work alongside our existing team or SI?
Yes. Independent design assurance works alongside — not instead of — your delivery team or system integrator. We provide the independent scrutiny they cannot provide of their own work.
Ready to discuss your programme?
All initial conversations are confidential. No obligation to proceed.
